Do you accept walk-ins?
Yes, we do accept walk-ins depending on artist availability. However, to ensure you get the best experience, we recommend booking an appointment, especially for custom or larger pieces.
How should I prepare for my tattoo appointment?
Make sure to be well-rested, hydrated, and eat a good meal before your appointment. Avoid alcohol and drugs at least 24 hours prior. Wear comfortable clothing that provides easy access to the area youâ€™ll be getting tattooed.
Is getting a tattoo painful?
Tattoo pain varies from person to person and depends on the area being tattooed. Some areas of the body are more sensitive than others, but our artists do their best to make you as comfortable as possible throughout the process.
How much does a tattoo cost?
Tattoo prices vary based on the size, complexity, and detail of the design, as well as the artistâ€™s time. We offer an hourly rate or flat fees for certain pieces. You can discuss pricing during your consultation.
What should I expect after getting a tattoo?
After your tattoo session, your artist will provide aftercare instructions. You may experience redness, swelling, or tenderness in the tattooed area for a few days. Proper aftercare is essential for healing and maintaining your tattoo's quality.
What is your studioâ€™s hygiene and safety process?
We follow strict hygiene standards, including sterilized equipment, disposable needles, and single-use ink caps. Our artists wear gloves throughout the process, and our studio is thoroughly cleaned to ensure a safe and sterile environment.
